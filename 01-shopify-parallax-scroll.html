Each layer scrolls at a different speed, creating depth from flat elements

Smooth Scroll

Lenis replaces native scroll with lerp-based interpolation. The page glides instead of snapping — momentum carries through after you stop.

Mouse Parallax

In the hero, mouse position is normalised to −1…+1, inverted, and applied per-layer with different speed multipliers. A 5% lerp per frame creates the cinematic drag.

Scroll Parallax

Below the hero, each layer's translateY is scroll position multiplied by a speed factor. Lower speed means less movement — the layer appears further away.

Scroll Reveal

IntersectionObserver adds a visibility class as sections enter the viewport. CSS transitions handle the fade-up — no animation library needed.
