Smooth Scroll

This page uses Lenis for physics-based momentum scrolling. Scroll down and feel the difference — it glides instead of snapping.

Momentum

After you stop scrolling, the page keeps moving and decelerates naturally. Like swiping on a phone, but on desktop.

Scroll Progress

Lenis exposes a normalised progress value (0–1) that you can use to drive animations, parallax, or timeline scrubbing.

Reveal on Scroll

Combine smooth scroll with IntersectionObserver to fade elements in as they enter the viewport. No animation library needed.

Drop It In

Lenis is ~4KB gzipped. One import, three lines of setup, and your entire page scrolls like a native app.
